SANDWICH GALORE

Description

Whether you are snowed in or need an easy snack, I have great combinations!

Ingredients (vary by sandwich)

  • Sliced Bread
  • Peanut butter (or almond butter as I prefer)
  • Jelly (strawberry is the best!)
  • Cream Cheese
  • Fluff!

Directions

  • Peanut Butter and Jelly
    1. spread peanut butter on one slice of bread
    2. repeat with jelly
    3. smash together
    4. take bite and enjoy
  • Peanut Butter and Cream Cheese
    1. spread peanut butter on one slice of bread
    2. repeat with cream cheese
    3. smash together
    4. take bite and enjoy
  • Peanut Butter and Fluff
    1. spread peanut butter on one slice of bread
    2. repeat with Fluff
    3. smash together
    4. take bite and enjoy
